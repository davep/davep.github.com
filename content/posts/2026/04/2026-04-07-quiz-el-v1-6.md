A quick little refresh of one of my old packages, this time
[`quiz.el`](https://github.com/davep/quiz.el). This is a nice little
distraction when you're working in [Emacs](/category/emacs/), letting you
spin up a quick trivia quiz in a buffer.

![Quiz in action](/attachments/2026/04/07/quiz.png)

It's backed by the [Open Trivia Database](https://opentdb.com/), so there's
a good few subjects, questions, and levels of difficulty to play with.

The only changes I've made to it in this release are the usual clean-ups of
the [deprecated uses of `setf`](/2022/08/23/i-must-be-getting-old.html),
plus I've added <kbd>q</kbd> as a binding to the quiz window to quickly quit
the quiz.

I might have to come back and revisit it soon, as it looks like the default
face choices could probably do with a rethink, and I can see at the moment
that the attempt at a window-wide "header" for each question isn't working
any longer. For comparison, here's how the package looked when running back
when I first wrote it back in 2017:

![How it originally looked](/attachments/2026/04/07/original-quiz.png)

Leaving aside the fact that I was [still running a very light Emacs
then](/2020/06/14/my-journey-to-the-dark-side-is-complete.html), those
question headers are displaying differently, with the background colour no
longer spanning the width of the window. I'd like to understand why.
