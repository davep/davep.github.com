After adding the [stats page](/stats/) to
[BlogMore](https://blogmore.davep.dev/)
[yesterday](/2026/03/24/blogmore-v2-3-0.html) I realised that the main
stylesheet was starting to get fairly large. Not so big that it was a
problem for downloading (and of course normally it would get cached anyway),
just more that it was carrying around styles for things that only appear on
one page (the styles for the stats, for example).

So [I decided to break it up](https://github.com/davep/blogmore/issues/339).
Now, as well as the main `style.css`, there's also:

- `archive.css` for the [archive pages](/archive/)
- `search.css` for the [search page](/search/)
- `tag-cloud.css` for the [category](/categories/) and [tag](/tags/) clouds
- `stats.css` for the [stats](/stats/)

This *should* keep the load times for the main pages and individual posts
just a wee bit faster when first encountered, leaving off all those styles
that aren't necessary.

All of which means, along with [a wee wording change on the stats
page](https://github.com/davep/blogmore/issues/342), [BlogMore
v2.4.0](https://blogmore.davep.dev/changelog/#v240) is in the wild and ready
to use.
