A quick update carrying on from [this morning's
release](/2026/06/06/blogmore-v2-40-0.html): as mentioned towards the end of
the post, there was a small problem with the [lint
command](https://blogmore.davep.dev/linting/) where it would raise an error
if a page or post linked to any page related to the new series feature.

[v2.40.1](https://blogmore.davep.dev/changelog/#v2401) fixes this. The
linter now knows about the series feature and the URLs it creates.
