After [adding floating and inline tables of contents in
BlogMore](/2026/06/04/blogmore-v2-38-0.html) the other day, it was time to
update [`blogmore.el`](https://github.com/davep/blogmore.el) to include
commands to toggle the related frontmatter.

So, with the release of [`blogmore.el`
v4.6.0](https://github.com/davep/blogmore.el/releases/tag/v4.6.0), two new
commands are now available:

- `blogmore-toggle-show-toc` toggles the `show_toc` frontmatter property.
- `blogmore-toggle-show-toc-inline` toggles the `show_toc_inline`
  frontmatter property.

Both have also been added to the transient menu so they're easy to discover
and use.
